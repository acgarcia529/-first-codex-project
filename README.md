# Ireland + Scotland Family Itinerary Workspace

This workspace is a planning source of truth for a family of 5 traveling from the U.S. to Ireland and Scotland from May 24 to June 3, 2026.

## Files

1. `trip.yaml` — structured source file with dates, travelers, route, bases, transportation assumptions, constraints, must-dos, and booking priorities.
2. `itinerary.md` — hour-by-hour day plan grouped by lodging base/region.
3. `restaurants_by_day.md` — 3 breakfast, 3 lunch, and 3 dinner options per trip day with links and reservation guidance.
4. `lodging_recommendations.md` — Marriott, Airbnb/Vrbo search guidance, aparthotels, and castle options by region.
5. `hidden_gems_and_photo_spots.md` — iconic stops, hidden gems, photo spots, teen ideas, and weather alternatives by region.
6. `driving_summary.md` — route logic, estimated drive times, rental car plan, and backtracking risks.
7. `rainy_day_backup.md` — region-by-region bad-weather swaps.
8. `booking_checklist.csv` — bookable items, priority level, notes, and source links.
9. `ireland_scotland_trip_calendar.ics` — importable calendar summary of the itinerary.
10. `executive_summary.md` — short family-shareable version of the final recommended trip.
11. `ireland_scotland_trip_email.eml` — ready-to-open email draft addressed to `acgarcia1989@hotmail.com` with the workspace files attached.
12. `README.md` — this guide.

## How to Update the Plan

1. **Start with `trip.yaml`.** Update locked facts first: flight times, confirmed lodging, car pickup/drop-off, restaurant reservations, and attraction ticket times.
2. **Adjust `itinerary.md`.** Keep the same pacing rules: no tight plans after landing, no rental car before Scotland, no long drive on June 3, and flag days over 3.5 hours of driving.
3. **Update restaurants.** In `restaurants_by_day.md`, replace options with confirmed reservations and remove any places that are closed, fully booked, or menu-inappropriate. Do not invent hours or availability.
4. **Update lodging.** In `lodging_recommendations.md`, move confirmed stays to the top of each region and add cancellation deadlines.
5. **Update `booking_checklist.csv`.** Change `Status` from `Verify/book` to `Booked`, `Reserved`, `Cancelled`, or `Not needed` as decisions are made.
6. **Update `executive_summary.md` last** so the family-facing version reflects the final chosen route and bookings.
7. **Regenerate `ireland_scotland_trip_calendar.ics` manually** if exact flight times, ticket times, or dinner reservations change. Calendar times are placeholders until bookings are confirmed.
8. **Regenerate `ireland_scotland_trip_email.eml`** after major edits so the attached files match the latest workspace.

## QA Rules to Preserve

- Scotland stays at the front of the trip: Edinburgh May 25-27.
- Fly Dublin to Edinburgh and fly Edinburgh back to Ireland; do not use ferry/coach unless flights fail.
- Pick up the rental car only after returning from Scotland.
- Start the Ireland road trip in Belfast if the rental economics work.
- Verify Belfast pickup / Dublin Airport drop-off one-way and cross-border fees before booking.
- Keep the castle stay between West Ireland and Kerry.
- Keep the final night near Dublin Airport for the June 3, 2026 11:00 AM return flight.
- Do not overload the long driving days: Belfast to Galway, Dingle day, and Killarney to Kilkenny.
- Treat Black Cab Belfast as an optional swap, not an add-on to Titanic Belfast, unless the flight lands early and everyone has energy.

## Verification Notes

This workspace intentionally avoids invented opening hours, prices, flight schedules, and Airbnb availability. The links are planning sources; verify every booking directly with the airline, hotel, restaurant, attraction, or rental car company before paying.
