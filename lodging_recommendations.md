# Lodging Recommendations

Do not treat this as live availability. For Marriott/Aparthotel/castle sites, verify rooms for 5 people, bed types, cancellation policy, parking, breakfast, and May/June 2026 pricing before booking. For Airbnb/Vrbo, use search guidance unless you can verify a specific listing's live availability directly.

## Edinburgh — May 25-27, 2 nights

### Marriott options
- **W Edinburgh / St James Quarter** — best if budget allows; walkable New Town shopping, tram access, and easy taxis to Old Town. Parking is difficult/expensive; do not bring a car. Source: https://www.marriott.com/
- **Courtyard Edinburgh** — practical Marriott-family option near New Town/Calton Hill; confirm family room configuration. Source: https://www.marriott.com/
- **Moxy Edinburgh Fountainbridge** — fun teen-friendly vibe, often better value; longer walk/taxi to Castle/Royal Mile. Source: https://www.marriott.com/

### Aparthotel options
- **Native Edinburgh** — New Town apartment-style base; verify sleeps-5 units. Source: https://nativeplaces.com/properties/native-edinburgh/
- **Staycity Aparthotels Edinburgh** — kitchen/laundry style can help families; verify exact location and 5-person units. Source: https://www.staycity.com/edinburgh
- **Fraser Suites Edinburgh** — Old Town location; verify larger rooms/suites. Source: https://www.frasershospitality.com/

### Airbnb/Vrbo search guidance
Search **Old Town near Grassmarket/Victoria Street** or **New Town near St Andrew Square/George Street**. This reduces taxi time because the itinerary clusters around Castle, Royal Mile, Camera Obscura, Chocolatarium, Princes Street, and Calton Hill. Filters: entire home/apartment, sleeps 5, 2+ bedrooms, ideally 2 bathrooms, washer/dryer, elevator if upper floor, strong recent reviews, flexible cancellation, no car parking required. Parking in central Edinburgh is difficult.

## Belfast — May 27-28, 1 night

### Marriott options
- **AC Hotel Belfast** — riverside/Cathedral Quarter access; convenient for Titanic Quarter and dinner. Verify family rooms or connecting rooms. Source: https://www.marriott.com/

### Aparthotel options
- **room2 Belfast Hometel** — apartment-like rooms and central location; verify 5-person setup. Source: https://room2.com/belfast/
- **Staycity Aparthotels Belfast City Centre** — practical for families and parking checks. Source: https://www.staycity.com/belfast

### Airbnb/Vrbo search guidance
Search **Cathedral Quarter**, **Titanic Quarter**, or **City Hall/Linen Quarter**. This keeps Titanic Belfast, Black Cab pickup, dinner, and the airport/rental car route efficient. Filters: sleeps 5, 2 bathrooms if possible, washer/dryer, parking or nearby secure parking, elevator, walkable restaurants. Belfast city parking can be tight; confirm parking before booking.

## Galway / Salthill — May 28-29, 1 night

### Marriott options
Marriott-branded coverage is limited in Galway; search Marriott Bonvoy first, but expect better fits from local hotels/aparthotels.

### Hotel/aparthotel options
- **The Galmont Hotel & Spa** — central Galway, easier city access; verify family room/parking. Source: https://www.thegalmont.com/
- **Salthill Hotel** — good for Prom/beach access and easier parking than city core; verify family rooms. Source: https://www.salthillhotel.com/
- **Jameson Court Apartments, Salthill** — apartment-style family base; verify availability and 2-bath layouts. Source: https://www.jamesoncourt.ie/

### Airbnb/Vrbo search guidance
Best search areas: **Salthill** for promenade/parking and easy westward exit, or **Galway West End** for dinner/music walkability. Filters: sleeps 5, 2+ bedrooms, ideally 2 baths, washer/dryer, parking included, walkable dinner options, flexible cancellation. Galway city-center parking is difficult; Salthill often reduces stress.

## Castle Stay — May 29-30, 1 night

### Recommended castle night
- **Dromoland Castle, County Clare** — best fit between Galway/Cliffs of Moher and Kerry. It turns the transfer into a special night rather than a detour. Book early for family configuration; reserve dinner/estate activities. Source: https://www.dromoland.ie/

### Castle backups
- **Glenlo Abbey Hotel & Estate, Galway** — excellent if you prefer less movement after Galway, but it is less ideally placed for the Kerry transfer. Source: https://www.glenloabbeyhotel.ie/
- **Ballyseede Castle, Tralee** — atmospheric and closer to Kerry, but it pushes more driving after the Cliffs/Galway day. Source: https://www.ballyseedecastle.com/

### Airbnb/Vrbo note
Do not replace the castle night with Airbnb unless castle pricing/availability fails. If needed, search **Ennis**, **Bunratty**, or **Newmarket-on-Fergus** for a practical one-night house with parking.

## Killarney / Kerry — May 30-June 1, 2 nights

### Marriott options
Marriott-branded options are limited in Killarney/Kerry. Check Marriott Bonvoy but prioritize local family hotels/aparthotels if no suitable family room exists.

### Hotel/aparthotel options
- **The Killarney Park** — central luxury; verify family rooms/connecting rooms. Source: https://www.killarneyparkhotel.ie/
- **Great Southern Killarney** — central, classic, rail-station area; parking to verify. Source: https://www.greatsouthernkillarney.com/
- **The Gleneagle Hotel & Apartments** — strong family/apartment-style option with parking and activities; slightly outside the town center. Source: https://www.gleneaglehotel.com/

### Airbnb/Vrbo search guidance
Search **Killarney town center** or **Muckross Road**. Town center gives walkable dinners/music; Muckross Road positions you toward National Park and Dingle route. Filters: sleeps 5, 2 bathrooms, washer/dryer, free parking, quiet bedrooms, walkable/taxi-easy dinner. Parking is easier than central Edinburgh/Galway but still verify.

Marriott-branded coverage is limited; use local hotels/aparthotels.

## Dublin Airport Area — June 2-3, final night

### Marriott options
- **Aloft Dublin City** — Marriott option in city rather than airport; only choose if you return car early and are comfortable with morning taxi. Source: https://www.marriott.com/
- **The Shelbourne / The Westin Dublin / The Morrison** — Marriott-family city options but not ideal for final 11:00 AM airport departure. Source: https://www.marriott.com/

### Airport hotel options
- **Radisson Blu Hotel Dublin Airport** — strongest final-night airport convenience. Source: https://www.radissonhotels.com/
- **Maldron Hotel Dublin Airport** — practical airport hotel. Source: https://www.maldronhoteldublinairport.com/
- **Carlton Hotel Dublin Airport** — airport-area option; verify shuttle/family rooms. Source: https://www.carltondublinairport.com/

### Airbnb/Vrbo search guidance
For the final night, hotel is better than Airbnb. If using a rental, search **Swords** or **Malahide** only if parking is guaranteed and airport transfer is simple. Filters: sleeps 5, parking, no complicated self-checkout, 15-20 minutes to airport in morning traffic. Final-night parking and shuttle reliability matter more than charm.
