# Rainy-Day Backup Plans by Region

## Edinburgh
- Camera Obscura & World of Illusions.
- The Chocolatarium.
- National Museum of Scotland.
- Scottish National Gallery + The Scottish Café.
- St James Quarter shopping/cinema-style downtime.

## Belfast / Northern Ireland
- Titanic Belfast.
- Ulster Museum and Botanic Gardens Palm House.
- St George's Market if operating.
- Victoria Square dome/shopping.
- Black Cab tour only as a Titanic swap if the family wants history more than Titanic; otherwise keep the evening easy.

## Galway / Salthill / West Ireland
- Galway City Museum.
- Charlie Byrne's Bookshop and Latin Quarter cafés.
- Galway Atlantaquaria in Salthill.
- Salthill cinema/bowling-style downtime.
- If Cliffs weather is unsafe, swap to Aillwee Burren Experience or Bunratty Castle & Folk Park.

## County Clare Castle Night
- Castle lounges, afternoon tea, spa/pool if available.
- Bunratty Castle & Folk Park.
- Aillwee Burren Experience.
- Ennis cafés/shops.
- Estate activity only if weather and availability allow.

## Killarney / Dingle / Kerry
- Muckross House.
- Killarney House & Gardens indoor sections/café.
- Dingle Oceanworld Aquarium.
- Killarney town cafés/shops.
- Short Torc Waterfall walk only if safe; skip exposed Slea Head viewpoints in poor visibility.

## Kilkenny / Rock of Cashel
- Kilkenny Castle interiors.
- Medieval Mile Museum.
- Smithwick's Experience.
- Cakeface / café crawl.
- Rock of Cashel can still work in light rain, but skip exposed Hore Abbey if wind/rain is severe.

## Dublin / Airport Area
- EPIC The Irish Emigration Museum.
- National Gallery of Ireland.
- Little Museum of Dublin.
- Powerscourt Townhouse Centre / George's Street Arcade.
- Airport hotel downtime and early packing.
